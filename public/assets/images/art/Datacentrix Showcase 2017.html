Justin Hamman / Account Manager, Datacentrix

The importance of the environment is key to the entire South African financial sector as it impacts on the processing of all card transactions in SA. The reason Bankserv chose to transact through DCX is because of our commitment to develop a local support base of Nonstop resources.
